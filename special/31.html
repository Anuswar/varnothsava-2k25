📜 About

A vibrant marketplace of student creativity and entrepreneurship! Whether you’re an artist, a tech enthusiast, a craftsperson, or a budding entrepreneur. This is your chance to turn your creativity into business! Showcase Your Talent – Display and sell your innovative products or services.

  • Gain Real-World Experience Learn business skills like pricing, customer engagement, and sales.
  • Network & Market Your Ideas Connect with students, faculty, and potential customers. Compete for the Best Stall Award!

📜 Rules & Guidelines

  • ✔️ All products/services must be created by students – no reselling of external items.
  • ✔️ Teams must manage their own stall setup and sales.
  • ✔️ Registration is on a first-come, first-served basis Open only to SMVITM students (Engineering & MBA).
  • ✔️ A total of 15 teams will be selected to set up stalls.
  • ✔️ Each team can have 1 - 4 members.
  • ✔️ Products/services can include handmade crafts, mehndi art, accessories, tech innovations, creative business ideas, or any unique student initiative.
  • ✔️ Participation certificates will be awarded to all teams

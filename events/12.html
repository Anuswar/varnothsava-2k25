About the Event

Electronics circuit-based hands-on event where teams answer the given questions and prepare the circuit by solving the errors in the existing circuit diagram.

Rules & Guidelines

  • No usage of mobiles, laptops, or any textbooks while the event is going on.
  • Asking for any type of help from another is prohibited.

Rounds

  • Round 1: A simple pen-and-paper test with 25 questions on electronics that must be answered in 30 minutes.
  • Round 2: Drawings of circuits with faults will be sent to the teams that advanced from Round 1. Their job is to find the errors and then precisely redo the circuit.
  • Final Round: Participants in this round must design and construct a circuit hands-on and demonstrate the intended output. The top qualifiers from Round 2 will be permitted to compete.

About the Event

A team-based machine learning challenge where participants spin a wheel to receive a random algorithm, preprocess datasets, and build models within a time limit.

Rules & Guidelines

  • Teams spin a wheel to receive a random machine learning algorithm.
  • Teams preprocess the provided dataset and apply the assigned algorithm.
  • Present solutions to judges in the final round.
  • Participants must submit their predictions within the given time limits.
  • Use of the internet is restricted except for documentation purposes.

Rounds

  • Round 1: Spin the wheel for a random ML algorithm, preprocess & apply it to a dataset (30 min); top teams advance based on accuracy & insights.
  • Final Round: Refine models, present to judges, answer rapid-fire questions, and showcase creativity.
